Process

Customized certificates

You can upload background images for editing or upload PDF files for encryption directly. We provide a variety of certificate templates and a comprehensive character set font to address design and rare character display issues.

Rapid winding

We are certified to comply with GDPR and ISO/IEC 27001. We adapt the W3C DID standard and EU Sustainable Blockchain EBSI IOTA for sharing, enabling organizations to swiftly digitize their paper-based documents.

Certificate management

You can easily manage certificate delivery status, set issuance dates and quantities for recipients in diverse groups, instantly revoke or reissue certificates for errors or content adjustments, all in real-time.

One-click verification

You can verify the authenticity of the certificates with just one click through the LinkedIn link or QR code on the paper document. No need to worry about certificate counterfeiting or tampering and establish cross-border trust transmission.
